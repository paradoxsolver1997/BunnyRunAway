## ğŸ¯ How to Play

### Objective
Prevent the bunny from reaching any of the four corner holes by strategically placing barriers. The bunny will always try to find the shortest path to escape!

### Game Elements
- ğŸ° **Bunny**: Your clever opponent that uses intelligent pathfinding
- ğŸš§ **Barriers**: Place these to block the bunny's path (limited number available)
- ğŸ•³ï¸ **Holes**: The four corner exits the bunny is trying to reach
- ğŸŒ³ **Decorations**: Trees and stones that add visual appeal but don't affect gameplay

### Basic Gameplay
1. **Study the Map**: Look at the layout and plan your barrier placement
2. **Place Barriers**: Click on empty spaces to place barriers (you have a limited number)
3. **Start the Game**: Click the "Start" button to begin the bunny's movement
4. **Watch & React**: The bunny will move toward the nearest hole
5. **Strategic Placement**: Place barriers to create dead ends and force the bunny into traps
6. **Win Condition**: Completely surround the bunny with no escape path

### Tips for Success
- **Think Ahead**: Plan multiple moves in advance
- **Create Dead Ends**: Force the bunny into areas with no exit
- **Use Limited Barriers Wisely**: Every barrier counts!
- **Watch the Bunny's Path**: The bunny always chooses the shortest route
- **Practice on Easy Maps**: Master the basics before tackling Hard difficulty

### In-Game Tutorial
- In the game panel, click the *Tutorial* button to open the tutorial
- Collapse the tutorial and expand it during the game for quick reference

### Beginner Tips

2. **Watch the bunny's movement** carefully to understand pathfinding
